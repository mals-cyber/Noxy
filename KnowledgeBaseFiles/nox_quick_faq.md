## FIRST DAY ESSENTIALS

**Q: What time should I arrive on my first day?**
A: Report to the HR Department at 8:00 AM. Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to allow time for registration.

**Q: What should I bring on my first day?**
A: Bring all required documents (IDs, NBI clearance, educational credentials, government forms), a pen and notebook, and a professional attitude!

**Q: Where do I go when I arrive?**
A: Go directly to the HR Department in the office where you were hired (Cebu or Manila office). Security or reception can direct you.

**Q: What's the dress code for the first day?**
A: Business casual or smart casual. Dress professionally to make a good first impression.

## DOCUMENTS & REQUIREMENTS

**Q: What are the absolutely required documents for Day 1?**
A: Minimum requirements: 2 valid government IDs, NBI/Police clearance, diploma and transcript (certified copies), and birth certificate. Other documents can follow within the grace period.

**Q: What if my NBI clearance is still processing?**
A: Inform HR immediately. You may be given a grace period to submit it, but this must be clarified before your start date.

**Q: Do I need original copies of my documents?**
A: For viewing purposes, yes. However, you'll submit photocopies or certified true copies. Keep your originals safe.

**Q: What is a certified true copy?**
A: A photocopy stamped and signed by the issuing institution (school, PSA, etc.) certifying it's a true copy of the original.

## BENEFITS & COMPENSATION

**Q: When does my HMO coverage start?**
A: Day 1! You're covered from your first day of employment, even if you don't have your card yet.

**Q: Can I use my HMO immediately?**
A: Yes, you can use it even without the physical card. HR will provide a temporary authorization letter if needed.

**Q: How many HMO dependents can I add?**
A: Check with HR during benefits enrollment. Typically, you can add a spouse and children, subject to plan limits.

**Q: When is payday?**
A: Payday schedule will be provided during HR orientation. Your first pay may be prorated based on actual days worked.

**Q: How will I receive my salary?**
A: Via direct bank deposit. Ensure you provide correct bank details during onboarding.

**Q: When can I start using my leave credits?**
A: Leave credits typically accrue after a certain period (often after regularization). Check the employee handbook for specifics.

## USING NOX

**Q: What is NOX?**
A: NOX (N-PAX Onboarding Expert) is your onboarding assistant system featuring Noxy, an AI chatbot, and document management tools.

**Q: How do I access NOX?**
A: You'll receive login credentials on Day 1. Access NOX through the web portal using your company email and password.

**Q: What can I ask Noxy?**
A: Anything about onboarding, company policies, benefits, procedures, contacts, and general company information!

**Q: Is Noxy available 24/7?**
A: Yes! Chat with Noxy anytime, day or night, weekends included.

**Q: What if Noxy can't answer my question?**
A: Use the Request HR Consultation feature to connect with a human HR representative.

**Q: How do I upload documents to NOX?**
A: Log in to NOX, go to Document Management, click Upload, select your file, tag it correctly, and submit.

**Q: What file formats are accepted for document uploads?**
A: Common formats: PDF, JPG, PNG. PDF is preferred for documents. Maximum file size will be specified in the system.

**Q: How do I know if my documents are approved?**
A: Check your NOX dashboard for status updates. You'll also receive email notifications for approvals/rejections.

## PROBATIONARY PERIOD

**Q: How long is the probationary period?**
A: Typically 5-6 months, in accordance with Philippine Labor Law.

**Q: What happens during probation?**
A: Your performance will be evaluated regularly. You'll receive feedback and guidance to help you succeed.

**Q: Can I be terminated during probation?**
A: Yes, employment can be terminated by either party during probation, but N-PAX provides support and feedback to help you succeed.

**Q: What are the requirements for regularization?**
A: Consistent good performance, adherence to company policies, teamwork, and meeting your role's expectations.

**Q: When will I be evaluated?**
A: Typically at 30 days, 90 days, and before regularization. Your supervisor will schedule formal evaluations.

## EMERGENCIES & SAFETY

**Q: What do I do in case of fire?**
A: Activate the nearest fire alarm, evacuate using stairs (not elevators), and proceed to the designated assembly area.

**Q: Where are the emergency exits?**
A: Emergency exits and evacuation routes will be pointed out during your office tour. Floor maps are posted on each floor.

**Q: Who are the floor wardens?**
A: Floor wardens will be identified during safety orientation. They wear special vests during emergencies.

**Q: What if there's a medical emergency?**
A: Call for help immediately, contact security or first aid responders, or dial 911 for ambulance.

**Q: Is there a clinic in the office?**
A: Check with HR. Some offices have first aid stations or arrangements with nearby clinics.
